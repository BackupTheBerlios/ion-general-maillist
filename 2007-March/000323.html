I use the following to switch to a left handed configuration (with an
optical Microsoft wheel mouse)

 xmodmap -e "pointer = 3 2 1 4 5"

and the following when I want to switch back to right handed configuration:

 xmodmap -e "pointer = default"


Hope this helps.

Avi.
