Who knows xhkeys^1?

I have just [a couple of days ago] discovered xhkeys ... and sorry if everyone
already knew of it. It works like a beauty both with my BenQ notebook and
Ion. I have mapped those horror windows keys on the left and on the right of
the spacebar, the 'application key' [just on the right of the right windows
key] .., have now all the <windows + tab, windows + whatever, ...>, <shift +
windows + ...>, ... series of keys available; have even mapped the 'Internet
hotkey' and the 'email hotkey'.

It works great. Beautiful!
