On 2007-05-18, cothrige <cothrige at gmail.com> wrote:
> Surely this is possible, but for some reason I am overlooking it.  How
> exactly would I manage to cycle through all three of these windows?

The defaults provide no way of _cycling_ through them: the movement
is geometrical. RTFM:

   Bindings for tilings and tiled frames [mod_tiling]

       Mod1+P, Mod1+N, Mod1+Tab, Mod1+K Tab
              Go to frame above/below/right/left of current frame.
