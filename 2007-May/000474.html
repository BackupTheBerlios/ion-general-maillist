On 2007-05-15, Fabio Nigi - <nigifabio at gmx.it> wrote:
> how can i include script at the starting of ion3?

- make the ~/.ion3/ directory
- put the script in ~/.ion3/
- copy one of {/usr/local/etc,/etc/X11}/ion3/cfg_ion.lua in ~/.ion3/
- add the line

	dopath("script name") 

  at the end of ~/.ion3/cfg_ion.lua
