Hello,

I’m maintaining backports for Debian etch of Debian unstable ion3-*
packages : https://intrigeri.boum.org/debian/

I’ll update them reasonably quickly when Debian unstable packages are
themselves updated. They are built for i386, a bit later for powerpc,
and the source packages are of course available.

I’ll go on with this work as long as I materially can, until Ben or
another Debian developer takes responsibility of maintaining backports
on backports.org, volatile.debian.org, or wherever.

As strongly stated on my Debian packages page, these packages are
usually somehow outdated in regard to upstream (pre-)releases : they
are thus not supported upstream, so *please do not report any bug
about these packages to the Ion author*. Really. My Debian packages
page also explains the current RC-nature of ion3.
